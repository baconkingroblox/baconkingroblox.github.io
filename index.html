baconifyUI (formerly BaconOS)

What's baconifyUI?

baconifyUI is an AOSP (or lineageOS if developing AOSP is difficult) based custom ROM designed to be lightweight. It has essential features and a nice look.

What platforms will be supported?

baconifyUI will be available on Roblox as a simulator, GSI, and any android device accepted by us (custom ROM).

When will baconifyUI release as a custom ROM/GSI?

baconifyUI will be available as a custom ROM/GSI when I get 32GB RAM.
